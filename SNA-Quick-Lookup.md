- **Air:** Fly 100 (perfect), whirlwind, highest AC
- **Earth:** Earth glide (phase through stone), highest damage, burrow
- **Fire:** Fastest land speed, burn damage on attacks, immune to fire
- **Water:** Swim 90, vortex, drench (put out fires), water mastery (+1 atk/dmg in water)
