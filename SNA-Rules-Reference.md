## How Summoning Works

### Appearance
- Creature appears where you designate within range
- It acts immediately on your turn
- It attacks your opponents to the best of its ability
- You can direct it not to attack, to attack particular enemies, or to perform other actions via a **free action** on your turn

### Number of Creatures

| Spell Level Cast | From Same-Level List | 1 Level Lower | 2+ Levels Lower |
|---|---|---|---|
| SNA I | 1 creature | — | — |
| SNA II | 1 creature | 1d3 same kind | — |
| SNA III | 1 creature | 1d3 same kind | 1d4+1 same kind |
| SNA IV+ | 1 creature | 1d3 same kind | 1d4+1 same kind |

**"Same kind" rule:** When summoning multiple creatures from a lower list, they must all be the same creature.

### Duration & Dismissal
- Duration: **1 round per caster level**
- Dismissible: Yes (standard action)
- Creature disappears when reduced to 0 HP or when duration expires
- Summoned creature cannot summon or otherwise conjure another creature
- Summoned creature cannot use any teleportation or planar travel abilities

### What Summoned Creatures CAN Do
- Attack enemies (full attack actions if applicable)
- Use extraordinary abilities (Ex)
- Use supernatural abilities (Su)
- Use spell-like abilities (Sp) — **but each SLA used costs 1 round of duration**
- Flank
- Use aid another
- Take attacks of opportunity
- Use special attacks (trip, grapple, etc.)
- Move, charge, withdraw, etc.

### What Summoned Creatures CANNOT Do
- Summon or conjure other creatures
- Use teleportation or planar travel
- Cast actual spells (unless specifically noted)
- Use magic items

## Key Feats & Enhancements

### Augment Summoning (Core)
**Prerequisite:** Spell Focus (conjuration)

All summoned creatures gain:
- **+4 Str**
- **+4 Con**

This affects: HP, melee attack/damage, CMB, CMD, Fort saves, Str/Con-based skills. Recalculate accordingly.

#### Quick Augment Summoning Math
| Original Stat | Augmented |
|---|---|
| Str/Con bonus | +2 to modifier |
| HP | +1 per HD (from Con) |
| Melee attack | +2 |
| Melee damage | +2 |
| Fort save | +2 |
| CMB | +2 |
| CMD | +4 (+2 Str, +2 Con) |

### Superior Summoning (Ultimate Magic)
**Prerequisite:** Augment Summoning, caster level 3rd

When you summon more than one creature with a summon spell, add **+1** to the total number of creatures summoned.

### Versatile Summon Nature's Ally (feat, various sources)
Apply one of the following templates to any creature summoned with SNA:
- **Aerial** (fly speed)
- **Aqueous** (swim speed, water breathing)
- **Chthonic** (burrow speed, darkvision)
- **Dark** (cold resistance, see in darkness)
- **Fiery** (fire damage on attacks, fire resistance)

### Moonlight Summons (Ultimate Magic)
**Prerequisite:** Spell Focus (conjuration), ability to cast SNA

Summoned creatures are considered to be **silver** for purposes of overcoming DR. They gain **darkvision 60 ft.** and gain the **Diehard** feat.

### Starlight Summons (Ultimate Magic)
**Prerequisite:** Spell Focus (conjuration), ability to cast SNA, Moonlight Summons

Summoned creatures are considered to be **cold iron** for purposes of overcoming DR. They gain **Blind-Fight** feat and a +2 bonus to Perception and Stealth.

### Sunlight Summons (Ultimate Magic)
**Prerequisite:** Spell Focus (conjuration), ability to cast SNA, Moonlight Summons

Summoned creatures gain **searing light** effect on natural attacks (as if affected by sun metal). They are **immune to blinding and dazzling** effects.

## Celestial / Entropic / Fiendish / Resolute Templates

Summon Nature's Ally does **NOT** apply alignment templates by default (unlike Summon Monster). Creatures summoned via SNA are typically just the base creature.

**Exception:** Some SNA lists include specific outsiders or creatures with inherent subtypes (elementals).

## Tactical Reminders

### Action Economy
- **Casting:** 1 full round (begins casting, completes at start of next turn)
- **Directing:** Free action on your turn
- **Dismissing:** Standard action

### Positioning
- Creatures appear where you designate within range
- No two summoned creatures can be more than **30 ft. apart**
- Summoned creature cannot appear in an occupied space
- Summoned creature does not provoke AoO when appearing

### Concentration
- If you take damage during the round of casting, DC = 10 + damage dealt + spell level
- Must maintain concentration during the casting round
- Spell completes at the beginning of your next turn

### Multiple Summons Active
- You can have multiple SNA spells active simultaneously
- Each is a separate spell with its own duration
- Each group needs to be directed separately (but each direction is a free action)
- Summoned creatures share your initiative count
